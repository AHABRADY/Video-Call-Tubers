Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about VideoCallTubers? We've got answers.

How does VideoCallTubers act as a virtual study partner? +
By turning any YouTube study-with-me video into a persistent floating window, VideoCallTubers acts as a virtual "body double." This psychological effect provides social accountability, boosting concentration and keeping you focused on your work.
Is the extension fully free? +
Yes! VideoCallTubers is fully free and open-source. Since we host it on GitHub, you can inspect the code yourself. There are zero subscriptions, paywalls, or features blocked behind premium tiers.
Does it track my search queries or data? +
No. The extension operates entirely inside your local browser storage. We have no external databases or analytics tools. We also strictly use YouTube's privacy-enhanced embed domain to ensure no cross-site cookies are tracked on your system.
How does it handle tab switching without resetting? +
We developed a background state worker that tracks active active calls. When you switch workspace tabs, our content injector is already briefed on the status, letting your floating companion stay persistent and loaded on the exact same video timestamp!
