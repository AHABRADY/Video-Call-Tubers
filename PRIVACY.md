# Privacy Policy for VideoCallTubers

**Effective Date:** 2026

At VideoCallTubers, your privacy is our highest priority. We believe that what you do on your browser is your business. This policy explains how our extension handles your data (spoiler: it doesn't).

## 1. Zero Data Collection
The VideoCallTubers extension operates entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data, browsing history, or usage statistics.

## 2. No External Servers
The extension does not communicate with any external servers owned by VideoCallTubers. Everything runs seamlessly within your browser's local environment.

## 3. YouTube Embeds & No-Cookies
To provide the video functionality, VideoCallTubers embeds YouTube videos. To protect your privacy, we strictly utilize YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode (`youtube-nocookie.com`). This ensures that YouTube does not store tracking cookies in your browser simply from loading the embedded widget.

## 4. Local Storage
We use your browser's local storage mechanism (`chrome.storage.local`) exclusively to save your user preferences (such as the last used video URL and your chosen buddy name) so you don't have to retype them every time. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser data.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in this repository.
